My Tips

I don’t have an actual camera yet, so I use a digital app on my phone. I mainly use the app “Old Roll”.
  • Always look for natural light.
  • Find the right camera or mode for different settings.
  • Experiment with angles and framing.
  • Look for symmetry, center points, or nature.
  • I don’t edit my photos much, but I’m starting to learn.
